# AI Meditation Companion

An AI meditation companion that understands your presence and adapts the experience instead of asking you to configure it.

## The Problem

Most meditation apps begin with choices:

- How long?
- Which music?
- Which meditation?

Ironically, users have to think before they can relax.

## Our Solution

Instead of asking users to configure meditation, our AI companion quietly welcomes them, creates a calming atmosphere, and stays present throughout the session.

The goal is to make meditation feel less like operating an app and more like being with a thoughtful companion.

## Prototype Features

- Minimal, distraction-free interface
- Ambient meditation experience
- Camera-ready architecture
- AI companion interaction
- Reflection journey card

## Future Vision

We are exploring a new interaction model where AI understands presence instead of waiting for clicks.

Future versions will integrate browser-based perception to personalize guidance based on user attention and presence.
